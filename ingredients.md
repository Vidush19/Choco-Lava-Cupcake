Dry Ingredients

1/2 cup All Purpose Flour
1/2 cup + 2 tbsp Castor sugar or bhura (not powdered sugar)
1/4 cup Milk Powder
A pinch of Salt
1/4 tsp baking powder
5 tbsp cocoa powder

Wet Ingredients

130 g dark chocolate
1/4 cup butter (55 g)
1/2 cup + 1 to 2 tbsp Hot water (1 tbsp is to adjust consistency)
